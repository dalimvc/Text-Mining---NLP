• All models have OK but not great accuracy. SVM performs slightly better here than other models.
  • When it comes to other metrics such as precision, recall and F1 score, CNN performs much better than any other model, with KNN performing the worst.
  • There were no "neutral" tweets in this data set. That's why classification was either to positives or negatives.
  • SVM was best at predicting positives as positive and also negatives as negatives, while KNN was the worse.
